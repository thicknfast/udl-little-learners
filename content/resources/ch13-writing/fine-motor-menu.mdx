## Fine Motor Menu

This menu of fine motor activities gives you a collection of options to build the hand strength and coordination students need for writing. Use it to plan centers, arrival activities, or quick movement breaks.

### What Is Included

- Activities organized by the skills they develop
- Options for different materials and interest levels
- Ideas that can be set up quickly with common classroom supplies
