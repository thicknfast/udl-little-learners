## Graphic Organizer Templates

These Google Slides graphic organizer templates help scaffold the writing process for early learners. Graphic organizers give students a visual structure for organizing their ideas before they write, reducing the cognitive load of facing a blank page.

### How to Use

- Print for hands-on planning or use digitally
- Offer multiple organizer options so students can choose the format that works best for them
- Model how to use each organizer during a whole-group mini-lesson before making them available independently
