## Q-Tip Fine Motor Activity

This Instagram reel demonstrates a Q-tip fine motor activity that helps students develop the hand control and precision needed for writing. Using Q-tips to paint, dot, or trace builds the same fine motor pathways that support letter formation.

### How to Use in Your Classroom

- Set up as a fine motor center with simple patterns or letter templates
- Pair with painting or dot art activities for creative expression
- Use as a calming activity that also builds writing readiness
