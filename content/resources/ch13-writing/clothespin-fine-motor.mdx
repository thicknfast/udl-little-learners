## Clothespin Fine Motor Activity

Watch this Instagram reel for a simple clothespin activity that builds the fine motor strength students need for writing. Fine motor activities like this one help develop the hand muscles and coordination that make holding a pencil and forming letters easier.

### Why Fine Motor Matters for Writing

- Students who struggle with hand strength often avoid writing tasks
- Building fine motor skills removes a physical barrier to written expression
- Activities like this can be set up as a center or used during arrival time
