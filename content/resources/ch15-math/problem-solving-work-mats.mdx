## Problem Solving Work Mats

These work mats give students a structured space for working through math problems. By providing designated areas for different steps in the problem-solving process, they reduce cognitive load and help students organize their thinking.

### How to Use

- Print and laminate so students can use dry-erase markers
- Model how to use each section of the mat during a whole-group lesson
- Place at math centers alongside manipulatives and other tools
