## Communication Buttons — Bunny Example

Watch this video of Bunny the dog using recordable communication buttons to express needs and ideas. While this is an animal example, it powerfully demonstrates how simple recordable buttons can support communication and expression.

### Classroom Connection

- Recordable buttons can be placed in centers so students can ask for help, share what they made, or request materials without needing to interrupt instruction
- They support students who are developing verbal language or who are nonverbal
- They add an element of independence and fun to center time
