## Task Card Examples

This Google Doc contains ready-to-use task card examples for different center types. Task cards tell students what to do at a center without needing an adult, making centers more self-sufficient and freeing you to work with small groups.

### How to Use

- Print and laminate for durability
- Display at each center alongside necessary materials
- Include visuals and simple text so all learners can access them
- Rotate task cards to keep centers fresh
