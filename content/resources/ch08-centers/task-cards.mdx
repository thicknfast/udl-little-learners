Here are some examples of what task cards could look like across different center types.

## Blocks Center

- "Build a tower as tall as you!" (with picture of child next to tall tower)
- "Create a bridge that a car can drive under" (with picture)
- "Build something with only triangles and rectangles" (with pictures of those shapes, great for magnetiles)
- "Make a house for our class bear" (picture of a house of blocks with a bear inside or other stuffy)
- "Build a maze for the small cars" (picture of a maze)
- "Create a pattern: long block, short block, long block..."

## Math Games / Math Manipulatives Center

- "Make groups of 5" (with picture of 5 bears, 5 blocks, etc.)
- "Roll the dice and build a tower with that many cubes"
- "Sort the buttons by color, then count each group"
- "Create a repeating pattern with 2 colors"
- "Use the balance scale - can you make both sides equal?"
- "Count out 10 objects. Now split them into 2 groups. How many ways can you do this?"

## Writing Center

- "Draw and label 3 things you see in our classroom"
- "Write a letter to a friend"
- "Make a list of your favorite foods"
- "Create a story about the picture" (with story starter image)
- "Write your name 5 different ways" (big, small, fancy, rainbow)
- "Make a card for someone special"

## Art Center

- "Paint a self-portrait"
- "Use only warm colors today" (with color examples)
- "Create texture rubbings with different objects"
- "Mix 2 colors - what do you get?"
- "Draw your family"
- "Create a collage using only torn paper"

## Science / Discovery Center

- "Sink or float? Test 5 objects and record your predictions"
- "Use the magnifying glass to observe the leaves - draw what you see"
- "Sort rocks by size/color/texture"
- "How many drops of water can fit on a penny?"
- "Build a ramp - which objects roll? Which slide?"
- "Plant seeds and observe them daily"

## Dramatic Play

- "Be a chef and create a menu for today"
- "You're a doctor - give the baby doll a checkup"
- "Set the table for 4 people"
- "Go shopping and write a shopping list first"
- "Be a teacher - read a story to the stuffed animals"

## Literacy / Word Work Center

- "Build 5 CVC words with magnetic letters"
- "Sort picture cards by beginning sound"
- "Find 3 objects in the room that start with B"
- "Match uppercase and lowercase letters"
- "How many words rhyme with 'cat'? Use the picture cards"
- "Rainbow write your spelling words"

## Puzzle Center

- "Complete a 12-piece puzzle"
- "Try a puzzle you've never done before"
- "Work with a partner to finish a puzzle together"
- "Time yourself - how fast can you complete this puzzle?"

## Cars / Transportation Center

- "Park cars in order from 1-10" (numbers on roofs, parking spots)
- "Race cars down the ramp - which goes farthest?"
- "Match the letter on the car to the letter parking spot"
- "Create a traffic jam with at least 10 cars"
- "Drive your car along the color path" (matching car color to line color)

## Sensory Table

- "Hide 10 objects in the rice - can your friend find them all?"
- "Use tools to transfer water from one container to another"
- "How many scoops does it take to fill the container?"
- "Find all the objects that start with 'S'"

## Movement / Gross Motor Center

- "Balance on one foot for 10 seconds"
- "Walk on the balance beam forwards, then backwards"
- "Do 5 jumping jacks, 5 hops, 5 spins"
- "Throw the beanbag into the bucket - score points!"
- "Yoga poses: try tree, mountain, and downward dog"
