## Menu of Station Ideas

This Google Doc provides a menu of literacy station ideas that you can mix, match, and adapt for your classroom. Use it as a starting point to design stations that offer choice and support a range of learners.

### How to Use

- Browse the full list and star ideas that fit your current instructional goals
- Choose a mix of independent, partner, and movement-based options
- Rotate ideas regularly to keep stations fresh and engaging
