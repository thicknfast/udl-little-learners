## UFLI-Aligned Center Structure

This Instagram post demonstrates how to structure literacy centers that align with the University of Florida Literacy Institute (UFLI) phonics approach. It shows how to organize stations so students can practice skills independently while you work with small groups.

### Key Features

- Stations that reinforce the phonics skills being taught in whole group
- Clear visual instructions so students know what to do
- A structure that supports both accountability and student agency
