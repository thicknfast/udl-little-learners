## Movement-Based Literacy Centers

This Instagram reel shows creative examples of literacy centers that incorporate movement. For students who need to move to stay engaged, these centers remove the barrier of extended sitting while keeping the focus on reading and phonics skills.

### Why Movement Matters

- Many early learners need movement to maintain focus and engagement
- Movement-based activities support kinesthetic learners
- They reduce the behavioral challenges that come from asking young children to sit still for too long
