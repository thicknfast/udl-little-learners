## Student Data Tracker

Use this template to track student progress in reading and phonics. Simple data tracking helps you make informed decisions about small group instruction, station assignments, and which students need additional support.

### How to Use

- Update weekly or biweekly based on your assessments
- Use the data to form flexible small groups
- Share with students so they can see their own growth over time
