## Collection of Reflection Activities

This Google Drive folder contains a collection of reflection activities that can be used throughout the school day. Reflection is a powerful tool for helping students process their learning, recognize growth, and build metacognitive skills.

### What You Will Find

- Age-appropriate reflection prompts and questions
- Visual reflection tools for pre-readers
- Templates for different types of reflection activities
- Ideas for embedding reflection into existing routines
