## Early Childhood Developmental Milestone Chart

This developmental milestone chart from Responsive Classroom (Yardsticks) helps you understand what is age-appropriate for your students. Knowing where students are developmentally helps you design morning meeting activities that are engaging and appropriately challenging.

### Why This Matters for Morning Meeting

- Helps you choose greetings, activities, and sharing formats that match students' developmental stage
- Explains why some students struggle with certain expectations
- Supports your ability to predict barriers and plan proactively
