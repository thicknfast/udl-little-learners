## Give Spark Check-In Samples

These Canva-designed check-in samples from Vicky Brooks provide ready-to-use visual tools for classroom emotional check-ins. They can be printed and displayed in your classroom or used digitally.

### How to Use

- Display at your classroom entrance or gathering area
- Have students point to or place a marker on how they are feeling
- Use as a conversation starter during morning meeting or small group time
