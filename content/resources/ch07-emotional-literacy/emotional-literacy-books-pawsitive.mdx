## 15 Books to Introduce Your Calm Corner

This curated book list from The Pawsitive School Counselor features 15 children's books perfect for introducing and reinforcing your classroom calm corner. Read alouds are one of the most powerful ways to teach emotional literacy.

### How to Use These Books

- Read one during morning meeting when you first introduce your calm corner
- Keep a few in the calm corner itself as a calming strategy
- Revisit favorites when the class needs a reset or when new students join
