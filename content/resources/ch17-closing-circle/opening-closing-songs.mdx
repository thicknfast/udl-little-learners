## Opening and Closing Songs

This collection from Storytime Katie provides opening and closing songs perfect for bookending your school day. Songs create predictable, joyful transitions that signal the beginning and end of community time.

### Why Songs Work

- They create a predictable ritual that helps students feel safe
- They engage multiple senses and pathways for learning
- They build a shared classroom culture and sense of belonging
- A song that opens the day can be mirrored by one that closes it, creating symmetry
