## CASEL Signature Practices Playbook

The Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning (CASEL) has developed a set of Signature Practices that weave social-emotional learning into the fabric of the school day. This playbook provides practical strategies for welcoming and inclusion, engaging strategies, and optimistic closures.

### Connection to UDL

CASEL's Signature Practices align naturally with UDL by providing multiple means of engagement and building the kind of safe, connected classroom community where all learners can thrive.
