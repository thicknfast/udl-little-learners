## Video Classroom Walkthrough

Watch this video walkthrough of an early childhood classroom intentionally designed using UDL principles. Created by Denise Mouton, this tour shows how classroom spaces can be organized to support every learner.

### What to Look For

- How different zones are set up for different types of learning
- Visual supports and labels that promote independence
- Materials that are accessible and inviting to all learners
- How the environment reflects community and belonging
