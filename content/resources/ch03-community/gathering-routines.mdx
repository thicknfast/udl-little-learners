## Classroom Gathering Routines

Watch this quick video example of classroom gathering routines in action. Shared by @the_crafter_teacher on Instagram, this reel demonstrates how to bring students together in a way that builds community and sets the tone for learning.

### Why Gathering Routines Matter

- They create predictable, welcoming transitions into group time
- They give every student a way to enter the community space
- They build a sense of belonging from the very first moment of the day
